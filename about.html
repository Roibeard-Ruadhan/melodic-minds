MY STORY

When Words Fail.... Music Speaks

I’m Niamh, founder of Wembley Music Therapy Services. A HCPC registered Music Therapist based in North London - here to guide you through the services provided.

I truly care about my clients’ well-being and guarantee a service that is catered to the clients needs throughout individual and group music therapy sessions.

Since 2016, I have provided a number of therapeutic services from Ireland, specialising in Autistic Spectrum Disorder, Dementia, Communication Disorders and Intellectual & Physical Disabilities.

Now located in Wembley, why not get in touch with me today for more information about individual and group music therapy sessions.
